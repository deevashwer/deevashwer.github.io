Deevashwer Rathee


I am an incoming CS PhD student at UC Berkeley.

Currently, I am a Research Fellow (RF) at Microsoft Research (MSR) India. At MSR, I work closely with Dr. Nishanth Chandran, Dr. Divya Gupta, Dr. Aseem Rastogi, and Dr. Rahul Sharma. Prior to joining MSR as an RF, I also interned here for 6 months.

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi in 2020 with a Dual Degree (B.Tech. and M.Tech.) from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering. In the summer of 2019, I interned with Prof. Thomas Schneider at TU Darmstadt. Before that, I did an internship at Kyushu University with Prof. Masaya Yasuda and at Waseda University with Prof. Takeshi Koshiba.
